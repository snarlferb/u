### Unicode

  We should probably include information on UTF-8 and Unicode, but it should go in a separate file.
  
  Temporary notes will be found here; Example Reasons -> It may be preferable to follow those encodings
  specified by the Unicode consortium, as there may exist some slight differences between one vendor’s
  definition of an encoding and another.

  PostScript Unicode encoding does not quite mesh with the unicode standard. This is because PostScript
  works w/ glyphs and Unicode deals with characters, therefore PostScript sees no distinction between
  space and nobreak space.
